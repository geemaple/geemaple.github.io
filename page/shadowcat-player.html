Shadowcat Player

AI-powered subtitle generation, export, and progress-based navigation.

Ideal for language learners and video creators to enhance content.

Features:

  • AI-generated subtitles: Automatically generated for accuracy and convenience.
  • Subtitle export: Easily export subtitles for your own use.
  • Progress-based navigation: Jump to specific video sections by selecting subtitles.
